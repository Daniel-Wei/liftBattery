# LiftOps User Case: Maya Liu

# LiftOps 用户案例：Maya Liu

---

## English

Maya Liu is a 31-year-old natural lifter with 5 years of lifting experience.

She is in week 7 of a 12-week cut. Her goal is to reduce bodyweight while keeping squat and hip thrust performance as stable as possible. Her priority areas are back, glutes, and quads.

Current context:

- Day: Friday evening after a lower-body session
- Session RPE: 9 / 10
- Session duration: 75 minutes
- Session load: 675 AU
- Top set effort: RPE 9.5, around 0-1 RIR
- Fatigue: 4 / 5
- Sleep quality: 2.8-3 / 5
- Soreness: 4 / 5
- Bodyweight trend: about -0.9% per week
- Hunger: 4.2 / 5
- Cardio: 195 minutes completed against a 150-minute plan

What changes in the app:

- Training Load rises because session load is calculated from session RPE x duration.
- Recovery Readiness drops because fatigue, soreness, and poor sleep are all moving in the wrong direction.
- Effort / RIR moves into watch because her top set is close to failure.
- Cut Pressure Watch stays medium because bodyweight is dropping quickly while hunger and cardio are high.
- Tomorrow's training mode becomes "Lighter tomorrow" instead of Push.

Theory mapping:

- Session-RPE load: practical internal load estimate.
- RIR / RPE: resistance-training effort and proximity-to-failure marker.
- Wellness self-report: fatigue, sleep quality, soreness, stress, and mood are subjective monitoring inputs.
- Bodyweight rate: cut-context signal, especially when combined with hunger and cardio.
- Watch state: a review signal, not a diagnosis or command.

## 中文

Maya Liu，31 岁，自然训练者，有 5 年力量训练经验。

她正在进行 12 周减脂周期的第 7 周。目标是在降低体重的同时，尽量维持深蹲和臀推表现。当前重点肌群是背部、臀部和股四头肌。

当前场景：

- 时间：周五晚上，下肢训练后
- 训练课 RPE：9 / 10
- 训练时长：75 分钟
- 训练课负荷：675 AU
- 顶组努力程度：RPE 9.5，大约只剩 0-1 次余力
- 疲劳：4 / 5
- 睡眠质量：2.8-3 / 5
- 酸痛：4 / 5
- 体重趋势：约 -0.9% / 周
- 饥饿感：4.2 / 5
- 有氧：计划 150 分钟，实际完成 195 分钟

App 里的输出变化：

- Training Load 上升，因为训练课负荷来自 session RPE x duration。
- Recovery Readiness 下降，因为疲劳、酸痛和睡眠都在变差。
- Effort / RIR 进入观察状态，因为顶组已经非常接近力竭。
- Cut Pressure Watch 保持 medium，因为体重下降较快，同时饥饿和有氧都偏高。
- 明天训练模式从 Push 变成 “Lighter tomorrow”。

理论映射：

- Session-RPE load：实用的内部训练负荷估计。
- RIR / RPE：阻力训练中的努力程度和接近力竭程度标记。
- Wellness self-report：疲劳、睡眠、酸痛、压力和情绪是主观监控输入。
- Bodyweight rate：减脂语境中的观察信号，尤其要和饥饿感、有氧一起看。
- Watch state：提醒用户复盘的信号，不是诊断，也不是命令。
